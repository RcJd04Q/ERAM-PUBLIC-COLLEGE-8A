About Eram Public College

Eram Public College is committed to fostering a learning environment where students can excel academically, socially, and emotionally. With a legacy of over 30 years, we have nurtured thousands of students to become leaders in their chosen fields. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities provide the perfect foundation for students to thrive.

At Eram Public College, we believe in holistic education that not only focuses on academics but also on extracurricular activities, sports, and personal development. Join us to experience a dynamic, student-centered approach to learning!

Our Academic Programs

Primary Education

Our Primary Education program builds a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills. We create a nurturing environment where young minds can explore, inquire, and grow.

Secondary Education

Our Secondary Education program equips students with in-depth knowledge in core subjects and prepares them for competitive examinations. We focus on individualized learning paths to help students achieve their academic goals.

Higher Secondary Education

We offer specialized streams in Science, Commerce, and Humanities at the higher secondary level, preparing students for university education and professional careers.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to academics, we emphasize the importance of extracurricular activities such as sports, music, arts, and drama, helping students develop confidence and leadership skills.
